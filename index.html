Shared Server Cluster

It's really expensive to rent enough servers to provide triple redundancy, with automated fail-over while having servers geographically spaced to ensure people all over the world get a good, quick user experience. Most of the time this means only big companies with huge pockets can offer good quality experiences to their users.

Adding to this expense most servers are only using a small percentage of their resources most of the time. Research from Stanford University suggests that 30% of servers haven't performed a function in 6 months or more. Environmentally this is a catastrophe as more and more energy and money goes to waste keeping the lights on.

However transformative technologies such as Cluster Computing have created a fantastic opportunity for us to come together to build a shared computing cluster in a secure manner, enabling us to come together to share computing resources, reducing the cost and providing greater availability.

If you're interested in running your website or software on our cluster, become a member and we can work together to build the next generation server architecture for our community.
